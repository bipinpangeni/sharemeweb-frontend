The Best Free File Sharing Tool — Faster Than WeTransfer

ShareMeWeb is the fastest and most private way to share files between devices. Unlike traditional file sharing services that upload your files to cloud servers, ShareMeWeb uses WebRTC peer-to-peer technology to send files directly from your browser to the recipient's browser — with zero intermediary storage.

Whether you need to share files without uploading, transfer large videos, send documents between your phone and laptop, or quickly share photos with friends, ShareMeWeb makes it instant and private. Just select your file, scan a QR code, and the transfer begins immediately.

ShareMeWeb works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge — on Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS. No app download required, no account needed, completely free.
