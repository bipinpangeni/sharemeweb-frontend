Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025. We believe privacy policies should be readable. Here is exactly what we do — and don't do — with your data.

Plain English Summary: For our P2P tool, we do not store your files. Files transfer directly between browsers. For our Cloud Vault, files are encrypted, temporary, and auto-deleted based on your settings.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

For our standard Peer-to-Peer (P2P) transfers on the homepage, the following data is never collected, stored, or transmitted to our servers:

  • Your files. Files transferred via P2P never touch our servers. They flow directly from the sender's browser to the receiver's browser using WebRTC technology.
  • File names or contents. We have no technical capability to access file contents. The WebRTC DataChannel enforces DTLS encryption.
  • Account information. We do not require registration, email addresses, usernames, or passwords.

2. Information We DO Collect

2.1 Cloud Vault Uploads

If you choose to use our "Cloud Vault" feature, your files are temporarily uploaded to our secure Supabase database servers. These files are subject to the auto-expiry and download limits you set. Once a file reaches its download limit or time limit, it is permanently deleted from our storage buckets.

2.2 Session Tokens

When you initiate a P2P transfer, our signaling server creates a short-lived session token. This token does not contain any file data and expires automatically when the browser tab is closed.

2.3 Analytics & Advertising

Our Service is supported by display advertising. Third-party advertising providers (such as Google AdSense) may place cookies on your browser to serve relevant advertisements. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties except when required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please email us directly at bipinpangeni58@gmail.com or use our Contact page.
